OUR HISTORY

At Billiards , we genuinely believe that every child has the potential to succeed, and that our encouraging, balanced and caring approach can realize this. Our school with exceptional staff and superb facilities provides the foundation for outstanding achievement. Every student is valued as an individual and our ‘holistic approach’ provides multiple opportunities where our students can learn, have fun, and flourish.

Billiards International Islamic School is one of the endeavors of Billiards group of education to impart quality education in international standards while nurturing human values.
